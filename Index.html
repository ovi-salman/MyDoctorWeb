Real Time Health Solution


'My Doctor' Home Healthcare Services is a standard and certified healthcare service provider that will be located in will be based in Dhaka Metropolitan City and gradually we will cover other major cities such as Chittagong, Sylhet, Rajshahi, Khulna,Mymensingh etc. Initially We chose to operate in these cities because we know that our services will be in high demand due to the growing numbers of the population in these areas. We will also focus on developing the referral system for General Practitioner's in rural areas specifically targeting the Community Clinics of our country by establishing & developing through 'My Doctor' points. 'My Doctor' assures you a unique combination of premier health care. We offer health care services such as doctor at home, health checkup, window of opportunity, Non communicable disease care, nutrition & diet, Palliative care, physio & speech therapy, treatment & counseling for mental disturbance & drug addiction in a systematic way to individuals, families or communities.
